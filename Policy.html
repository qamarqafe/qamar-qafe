Policies

Deposits

All event bookings require a deposit to secure the date. Deposits are non-refundable.

Deposit Policy by Booking Timeline

Time Before Event Deposit Required Remaining Balance Due
Same Month Booking 75% of total Remaining balance due 1 week before the event
1–2 Months in Advance 50% of total Remaining balance due 1 week before the event
3+ Months in Advance 25% of total Remaining balance due 1 week before the event

Open Tab Method (Weddings Only)

The Open Tab payment method is available for weddings with more than 100 guests.

  • A deposit is still required to reserve the date.
  • Full payment is expected immediately after the event for all drinks served under the Open Tab.

Additional Notes

We recommend reviewing all policies carefully before booking. Contact us if you have questions regarding deposits, payment methods, or event-specific arrangements.
