About Us

Meet the Founder

I'm Miriam — the face behind Qamar Qafe, a Palestinian owned business based in Raleigh, NC. I’ve always loved the small joys that come with a great cup of coffee: the ritual, the warmth, and the way it brings people together.

What started as casual weekend brewing quickly turned into a passion project, then a licensed business. Qamar Qafe was born out of late-night ideas, support from loved ones, and a desire to create meaningful experiences through handcrafted drinks.

“Qamar” means “moon” in Arabic — and just like the moon, I wanted this business to feel calm, cozy, and quietly magical. Every latte, matcha, and floral foam topping is made with that energy in mind.

From weddings to baby showers to backyard brunches, I’m proud to bring Qamar Qafe to your moments that matter most.
